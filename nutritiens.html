Nutrition Tips for Your Period
Eating the right foods can help you feel better and manage symptoms during your period.

  • Best Foods for Your Period

    Include leafy greens (spinach, kale), berries, bananas, eggs, fish, yogurt, and nuts. These foods provide iron, vitamins, and healthy fats to support your body and energy levels.

  • Hydration Matters

    Drink plenty of water! Staying hydrated helps reduce bloating, fatigue, and headaches. Herbal teas (like ginger or chamomile) are also soothing choices.

  • Magnesium for Cramps

    Magnesium-rich foods (such as dark chocolate, almonds, pumpkin seeds, and avocados) can help relax muscles and ease period cramps.
