Mental Health Suggestions for Your Period
Support your emotional wellbeing during PMS and your period with these mindful practices.

  • Mindfulness for PMS

    Practice deep breathing, meditation, or gentle body scans to help manage PMS symptoms and reduce stress. Mindfulness can help you stay present and calm.

  • Journaling Emotions

    Write down your feelings and thoughts each day. Journaling can help you process emotions, notice patterns, and gain clarity during your cycle.

  • Connect with Others

    Reach out to friends, family, or support groups. Sharing your experiences and feelings can help you feel understood and less alone.
