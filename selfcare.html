Self-Care Suggestions for Your Period
Taking care of yourself during your period can help you feel more comfortable and relaxed.

  • Warm Baths for Relief

    Soak in a warm bath to relax your muscles and ease cramps. Add Epsom salts or a few drops of essential oil for extra comfort.

  • Rest When Needed

    Listen to your body and rest when you feel tired. Taking naps or simply relaxing can help you recover and feel better.

  • Pamper Yourself

    Treat yourself to something you enjoy, like a face mask, your favorite snack, or a cozy movie night. Small acts of self-kindness can boost your mood.
